What are your shipping options and delivery times? ▼

We offer multiple shipping options to get your premium sneakers to you quickly:

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days - R99
Express Shipping: 2-3 business days - R149
Free Shipping: On orders over R1000 anywhere in South Africa

International shipping is also available with varying delivery times and costs.

What is your return and exchange policy? ▼

We want you to be completely satisfied with your sneakers:

Returns: Accepted within 30 days of purchase for unworn items in original packaging with tags attached.
Exchanges: Available for size exchanges within 14 days of receipt.
Condition: Items must be in resellable condition with no signs of wear.
Refunds: Processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Note: Customized or special order items cannot be returned.

How do I know the sneakers are 100% authentic? ▼

At MoreSneakers, authenticity is our promise:

Direct Sourcing: All sneakers are sourced directly from authorized retailers and official distributors.
Quality Verification: Each pair undergoes thorough inspection before sale.
Authentication Certificates: Provided with premium and limited edition releases.
Money-Back Guarantee: 100% authenticity guarantee - if we can't prove authenticity, you get a full refund.

We stand behind every pair we sell and maintain relationships with brand representatives.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We offer multiple secure payment options for your convenience:

Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard
Digital Wallets: PayPal, SnapScan
Bank Transfer: EFT payments
Cash on Delivery: Available in select areas of Gauteng
Store Credit: From returns or exchanges

All online payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

Do you offer sizing advice and fitting guidance? ▼

Yes! Getting the right fit is crucial for sneaker comfort:

Detailed Sizing Guides: Available on each product page with brand-specific measurements.
Expert Advice: Our team can provide personalized sizing recommendations based on the specific brand and model.
Fit Notes: We include notes about how each model typically fits (true to size, runs small/large).
In-Store Fitting: Visit our Midrand location for professional fitting assistance.

Contact us with your usual size and foot measurements for personalized advice.
