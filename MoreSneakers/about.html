About MoreSneakers

Your trusted partner for authentic and stylish sneakers since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, MoreSneakers began with a simple mission: to provide stylish, comfortable, and affordable sneakers to everyone. What started as a small online store has grown into a reputable retailer with both online presence and a physical store in Johannesburg.

We specialize in offering the latest trends in footwear for men, women, and children, all while maintaining competitive prices and exceptional customer service.

Our team of sneaker enthusiasts is dedicated to helping you find the perfect pair that matches your style and needs.

Our Mission

"To provide stylish, comfortable, and affordable sneakers to everyone while delivering exceptional customer service and promoting sneaker culture in South Africa."

Our Vision

"To become South Africa's most trusted sneaker retailer, known for quality products, fair pricing, and community engagement."

Why Choose MoreSneakers?

  • 100% authentic products with quality guarantee
  • Competitive prices and regular promotions
  • Wide selection of brands and styles
  • Expert advice from sneaker enthusiasts
  • Fast and reliable delivery across South Africa
  • Excellent customer service and easy returns
